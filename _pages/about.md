Hi, I am a first-year Ph.D. student in Data Science at the [School of Data Science](https://sds.cuhk.edu.cn/en), The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen, supervised by Prof. Jin LIU. I obtained my bachelor's degree from the School of Statistics and Mathematics at Zhongnan University of Economics and Law, majoring in Data Science and Big Data Technology, under the guidance of Prof. Xiaobo SUN.

My research focuses on developing algorithms for genomic analysis, including spatial transcriptomics, histology image analysis, and cross-modal data analysis.
